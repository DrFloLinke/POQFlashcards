Preface

Welcome to Analysing Quantitative Data with R! The purpose of this module is to introduce you to key components of the statistical programming language R and to demonstrate some of the most commonly used methods than can be applied to preprocess and analyse quantitative data.

The module is divided into four parts. Please work through each part in your own time, and ensure to complete the exercises at the end of each chapter.

If you have any queries in the meantime, please contact me under F.Reiche@warwick.ac.uk

And now, I hope you enjoy the module!
